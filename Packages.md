# R Packages

## Intro
R has become an essential tool in biology, largely because of its powerful, domain‑specific packages. For anyone working in the life sciences, the first step is to look at Bioconductor, a curated repository of over 2,000 packages dedicated to the analysis of high‑throughput biological data, especially from genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics.

## Bioconductor 
1. Biostrings - It is useful for those working in DNA, RNA and protein sequences.
2. ShortRead - useful to read high throughput sequences
